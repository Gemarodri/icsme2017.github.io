Registratin Site

    Registration is now open at CVENT.

    This registration site is for ICSME 2017, SCAM 2017, and VISSOFT 2017. For Internetware 2017 and 2017 China Software Engineering Research and Industry Summit, a separate registration form will be needed. Please get updated information via ICSME 2017 website.

Note to Authors

    Each accepted paper must have at least one author registered at a professional member rate. Papers for which the only registered authors are students must have one of the students register at the full (i.e., non-student) rate. Note that this policy does not apply to doctoral symposium papers.

    The ICSME research track author registration deadline is 16 July 2017. The early-bird registration deadline is 30 July 2017.

    ICSME Registration Includes: ICSME attendance including Lunch Wednesday through Friday, Reception on Wednesday, Banquet on Thursday, ICSME (Including Doctoral Symposium) Proceedings.

    SCAM Registration Includes: SCAM attendance including Lunch Sunday and Monday, Dinner on Sunday, SCAM Proceedings.

    VISSOFT Registration Includes: VISSOFT attendance including Lunch Monday and Tuesday, Dinner on Monday, VISSOFT Proceedings.

    Doctoral Symp. Registration Includes: Doctoral Symposium Attendance including Lunch and Dinner on Tuesday.


    If you have any questions about the registration, please contact Alyssa Gennello (IEEE Event Associate, icsmereg@ieee.org) and Yijian Wu (ICSME 2017 Financial Chair, wuyijian@fudan.edu.cn).
